Virtually all our clients have security clearances with the government. The invasive nature of a sale process can be disruptive; we work diligently to limit as many distractions as possible to shoulder as many burdens as possible so the business owner can focus on growing the business while the process runs in the background.

We provide extremely detailed proprietary financial modeling to our clients, and, as a result, we understand the intimate details of their programs, customers and prospective customers and can efficiently describe (sell) these attributes to prospective buyers/investors.

We are also a lean, focused boutique. This means we are hyper-focused on our industry of choice (aerospace, defense, government services, government contracting), and are thus able to bring the personalized, efficient, detailed approach.

Having the persistence to properly prepare a client for market, and in some cases, wait for the proper time to hit the market (possibly timed with a critical contract win or new government regulation) allows us to get the best outcomes.

We prepare before the market ripens and having the patience to recommend waiting when it is warranted rather than jump on a sub-optimal deal.
